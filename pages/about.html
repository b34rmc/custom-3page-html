Changing Peoples Lives

Devpipeline is here to change peoples lives and fill the GAP. What does that mean? Well the GAP is unemployed software developers. In the first page we went over why they are unemployed and now we'll talk about how DevPipelines filling the gap. At Devpipeline they are focused on 3 main sections, each section is 15 weeks long. The first section is Coding Foundations. In this part of the course they teach you the basic skills and fundamentals of programming. This builds a strong foundation allowing you to be able to switch and learn new languages in a fraction of the time.

After the first 15 weeks you start working part time. This allows you to start getting real world work experience while learning. your schedule changes from 8-12 class to 8-12 work and 1-5 class. during the second part your now enrolled in the front-end course. This is where you learn the fundamentals of web development. By the end of this next 15 weeks you will be able to program in react and be able to spin up fully functional websites in no time!

The third 15 weeks is the Back-end course. This is where you learn how to verify users, Create users, and much more that's handled on the back end. This is what makes your apps, web logins and all that from getting hacked, if done right. Nothing is 100% secure but this course will teach you how to keep your apps and websites secure and up to standard. After this course you will be a competent jr. developer and well on your way to senior. Because you come our of this course as a jr. developer devpipeline is able to help you find a job out of the gate because you have work experience.
